    About

    My name is Coire Walker, and I perform solo under the moniker "Rough Seis", and also under "SEIS" for more involved band material. This website is for promotion of my solo material: instrumental guitar compositions drawn from Post Rock, Folk, Blues, and Progressive Rock. My intention has always been to write music that conveys adventure, not bound to any traditional theories or structures, but rather free to journey as it will. Performing and writing music has always felt like a privilege; sometimes I am the one who gets to catch the frequency of the moment, make it tangible, and deliver it to those around me.
