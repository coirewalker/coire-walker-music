    About

    My name is Coire Walker and I love to make music. Currently, my sound is instrumental, drawn from Post Rock, Blues, Progessive Rock, and other world influences. My intention is to write music that conveys adventure, not bound to any traditional theories or structures but rather free to journey as it will. Performing and writing music has always felt like a privilage--sometimes I am the one who gets to catch the frequency of the moment, make it tangable, and deliver it to those around me.
